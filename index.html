About Us

Born in Vancouver, BC, The Ticket Takers are a four piece Indie Rock band that bring high octane energy to catchy melodies and sultry lyrics.

Comprised of Hayden Althuizen (guitar/vocals), Walter Dingwall (guitar/vocals), Keaton Althuizen (bass), and Andrew Wozer (drums), the group formed in November 2023.

Be the first to hear this young up and coming act here; catch them at LanaLou’s Restaurant, Friday June 21st.
